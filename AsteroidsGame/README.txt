the game is played using 2 buttons. space will move your planet further away but reduce its speed, left shift will do the opposite. the goal is to survive as long as possible.
you get 10 health in the top left and your score is in the middle. colliding with brown asteroids will damage you by 1, the star will kill you completely.
green asteroids give a point bonus.
you can pause and unpause the game by pressing escape.
the game can be played multiple times without restarting.
